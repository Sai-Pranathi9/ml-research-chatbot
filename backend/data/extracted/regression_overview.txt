Abstract
We show how to determine whether two given real polynomial functions of a single
variable are Lipschitz equivalent by comparing the values and also the multiplicities of
the given polynomial functions at their critical points. Then we show how to reduce the
problem of R-semialgebraic Lipschitz classiï¬cation of Î²-quasihomogeneous polynomials
of two real variables to the problem of Lipschitz classiï¬cation of real polynomial functions
of a single variable, under some fairly general conditions.
